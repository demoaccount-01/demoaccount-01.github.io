Hello There

Our Stupid Reactions

Korbin Miles and Rick Segall are the creators of Our Stupid Reactions. We react to stuff and offer our stupid and not asked for opinions with a dash of humor. In January 2019, Our Stupid Reactions had 1,800 subscribers. Our videos reacting to trailers of films like Captain Marvel, Creed II, Aladdin, and Bird Box were getting anywhere between a few hundred to a couple of thousand views. Then, one of our subscribers requested our reaction to the trailer of Gully Boy, a hotly anticipated Bollywood movie about a street rapper in Mumbai. “Brace yourself,” someone posted. “Indian subscribers are coming!!!” And now here we are, with over 690k subscribers and over a quarter of a billion views.

What We Do

We are more than just a reaction channel

Interviews

Our highlights include an interview with the legendary Zakir Hussain. Recently we interviewed the famous bollywood personality but firstly a great actor, Nawazuddin Siddiqui. We have also interviewed directors such as Anurag Kashyap and Sujoy Ghosh.

Trailer Reactions

We react to popular movie and TV show trailers from India. We have already reacted to over 100 movie trailers from India, lots of which helped us to know more about actors from India and the kind of cinema India produces.

Film Reviews

Lots of the trailers to which we reacted, intrigued us so much that we watched the movies too. So we also reviewed the films. We both are professional actors, so we love to get introduced to a whole new world of cinema.
